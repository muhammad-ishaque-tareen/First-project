Our Story

Founded in 2025, Weather Cast is the product of a Web Technology project by two passionate BSIT 5th-semester students. What started as an academic endeavor has grown into a vision to make accurate and accessible weather information available to everyone.


Our journey began when we combined our skills in web development and a shared passion for innovation to create a reliable and user-friendly weather forecasting platform. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of weather technology while staying true to our goal of providing accuracy and accessibility.

Our Mission

At Weather Cast, To deliver accurate, reliable, and user-friendly weather information using modern web technologies, making forecasting simple and accessible for everyone.

Our Technology

We leverage modern tools and APIs to build a reliable and efficient weather platform:

  • Weatherstack API: Provides accurate and real-time weather data for forecasting.
  • HTML5 & CSS3: Ensures a responsive and visually appealing user interface.
  • JavaScript: Powers dynamic content and interactive features for better user experience.
  • Servlets: Handles server-side processing and integrates data efficiently.
